# Security Policy

We take the security of Solid Stats seriously.

## Reporting a vulnerability

**Please do not report security vulnerabilities through public issues, discussions, or pull
requests.**

Instead, use **GitHub Private Vulnerability Reporting**: open the **Security** tab of the
affected repository and click **Report a vulnerability**. If that is not available to you,
contact the maintainers privately at [aleksandr.afgan0r.pavlov@gmail.com](mailto:aleksandr.afgan0r.pavlov@gmail.com).

Please include:

- the affected repository and the version or commit,
- a description of the issue and its impact,
- steps to reproduce or a proof of concept.

We will acknowledge your report, investigate, and keep you informed of the resolution. Please
give us a reasonable window to release a fix before any public disclosure.

## Supported versions

Solid Stats 2 — [`server-2`](https://github.com/solid-stats/server-2),
[`replays-fetcher`](https://github.com/solid-stats/replays-fetcher),
[`replay-parser-2`](https://github.com/solid-stats/replay-parser-2), and `web` — is the
actively maintained generation. The legacy
[`server`](https://github.com/solid-stats/server) and
[`sg-replay-parser`](https://github.com/solid-stats/sg-replay-parser) projects are not actively
maintained and do not receive security updates.
